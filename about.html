About Statistic

History

Statistic was founded by five dedicated CS:GO gamers who both in their professional life as well in their spare time both teach and play semi professional Counter-Strike


Vision: A supportive community of older gamers, united by a love of counter strike.


Mission: To provide a fun and engaging platform for older gamers to connect, compete, and promote sportsmanship.
