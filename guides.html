Maps

Vertigo

de_vertigo is a bomb defusal map that was made by Valve and was added in CS:GO October 2012, Vertigo is an active duty map

After countless of updates, the map has finally started to be accepted by the community and players. The map is a slighty T-sided map and has a double layered format. Vertigo sees the teams take upon eachother on the top two floors of a skyscraper that is under construction.

Overpass

de_overpass is a bomb defusal map that was made by Valve and was added in CS:GO December 2013 in the Winter offensive update alongside de_cobblestone. Overpass is an active duty map

Overpass is unique, in that it is the only competitive CS:GO map that wasn’t introduced prior to Global Offensive. The uniqueness continues, as the map has the CT’s spawn on the inside of A-bombsite. Because of this, rotations are fast and efficient. Overpass is fairly CT sided, as the T’s have to take multiple areas on the map before they can actually push A-bombsite.

Mirage

de_mirage is a bomb defusal map that was made by Valve, and was added in CS:GO June 2013. Mirage is an active duty map.

Mirage follows all of the standard conventions of CS:GO maps, with four main areas of contact, one being an open Mid area. Mirage is CT-sided, but not by much. It’s the only original Counter-Strike map to not be full reworked. There is a large variety of ways to play the map on both sides, so no games will be exactly the same. The styles of both teams and how they choose to play become incredibly apparent on Mirage.

Inferno

de_inferno is a bomb defusal map that was made by Valve and was added in CS:GO September 2016. Inferno is an active duty map.

Inferno was re-skinned again back in 2016 with several gameplay adjustments. Even though the map has had a very interesting evolution over the years, it has kept its reputation as a heavy utility map. Inferno is historically CT-sided, but has recently come closer to even for both sides. The most iconic spot from the map is Banana.

Nuke

de_nuke is a bomb defusal map that was made by Valve and was added in CS:GO March 2015. Nuke is an active duty map.

Nuke has been changed many times over the course of the various different games, although the layout has remained nearly the same. Nuke is known for its difficult T-side that is hard to play, but has still remained one of the most popular maps.

Ancient

de_ancient is a bomb defusal map that was made by Valve and was added in CS:GO December 2020. Ancient is an active duty map.

Ancient takes place in an Aztec-themed Central American archaeological site, giving it a vivid lush look that helps it stand out from other maps in the pool. Both the A and B bombsite are labelled as ‘plazas’ on the map, with everything from tarpaulin to mosaics and monuments being thrown in to give the map more character.
